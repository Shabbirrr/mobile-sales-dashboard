# 📊 Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Problem Statement

Analyze mobile sales data to identify trends across cities, products, time, and payment methods to support business decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Sales transactions
* Mobile models & brands
* Customer ratings
* Payment methods
* City-wise sales

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 📈 Sales peak observed in mid-year (around July), indicating seasonal demand
* 💳 UPI is the most preferred payment method (~26%), showing strong digital adoption
* 📱 Top 3 mobile models contribute a significant share of total revenue
* 🌆 Major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore dominate overall sales
* 📉 Mid-week sales dip observed, with lowest sales around Wednesday
* ⭐ Majority of customer ratings are concentrated around mid to high range

---

## 📊 Features

* Interactive filters (Month, Brand, Payment Method)
* City-wise sales visualization (map)
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Payment method distribution
* Product performance comparison
* Customer rating breakdown

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory for top-performing models
